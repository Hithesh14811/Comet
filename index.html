Comet.
Pleased to meet you.

Comet is a next-generation browser built by Perplexity, designed for thinkers, researchers, and everyday explorers. Our niche? Intelligent browsing — transforming your curiosity into clarity with real-time answers, seamless search, and a distraction-free interface that respects your time and your data.

If you’re looking for a smarter way to browse — one that’s fast, private, and built for discovery, then you’re exactly where you need to be.

Laser-focused on 3 key pillars of experience.

01

Instant Answer

No more tab-hopping or keyword guessing. Comet integrates Perplexity’s AI-powered engine directly into your browsing flow — delivering concise, cited answers instantly. Whether you're researching a topic, comparing products, or chasing a rabbit hole, Comet keeps you informed without the noise.
‍

02

Minimalist Design

We believe less is more. Comet’s interface is clean, intuitive, and built for focus. No clutter, no ads, no distractions. Just you, your questions, and the knowledge you seek. It’s browsing, reimagined for clarity and speed.

03

Privacy
FIrst

Your data is yours. Comet doesn’t track your searches, sell your clicks, or build profiles. We’ve engineered every layer to prioritize user control, transparency, and ethical tech. Because trust should be built-in — not an add-on.
